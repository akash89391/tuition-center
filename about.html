Hybrid Class


 For KCC’s hybrid classes, blend the best of both online and offline learning by offering flexible schedules where students can attend classes either in-person or virtually. Structure the curriculum to ensure that both online and offline students receive equal attention, using live streaming for in-person sessions and interactive digital tools like whiteboards, screen sharing, and recorded lectures for remote learners. Provide comprehensive study materials, both physical and digital, along with regular assessments to track progress. Keep batch sizes small to offer personalized support, and ensure classroom spaces are well-equipped for seamless hybrid learning.
